## Overview
The social core is the product's reason to exist: the surfaces that turn a private 5×5 Board into a shared, witnessed game. Every Mark publishes to its Prompt's public, attributed Tally ("see who else got it") — the core differentiator over the printed card (ADR 0002) — while the Board's layout and progress stay private. The Feed streams Proofs and Moments the group watches together as the honor-system source of truth (ADR 0001), Doubts apply social pressure without ever gating a Mark, the Leaderboard ranks for fun, Claim Modes tune friction (never trust), and Share Cards drop retina wins into the group chat.
