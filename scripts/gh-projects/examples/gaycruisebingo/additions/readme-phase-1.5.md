## Phase 1.5 — Daily Cards

Phase 1.5 turns the single whole-cruise Board into a themed **Day Card per cruise day**. Each of the ten sailing days owns a date, port, and Theme, and unlocks a fresh 5×5 card at **8:00 a.m. ship time** (Europe/Rome); locked future days are visible as themed previews that tease the party's dress code. A scheduled Cloud Function stamps a **Day Snapshot** of the approved Prompt pool at each unlock, and a Player's card is lazily dealt from that snapshot on first open — no repeats across the cruise until the pool exhausts. The two non-party days become **Tutorial Days** — Welcome Aboard (embark) and So Long, Farewell (disembark) — dealt from curated, admin-only pools and framed as onboarding and goodbye rather than competition. New main-pool submissions now land **Pending** until an Admin approves them, so nothing untested reaches a live card, and bare Marks surface in the Feed as **Tally Cards**. Scoring stays one cruise-long Leaderboard plus a per-day First to BINGO honor, closing on a two-beat finale (a Day 9 last-call standings Moment, then a Day 10 freeze + podium). Canonical spec: [`plans/daily-cards-spec.md`](https://github.com/nathanjohnpayne/gaycruisebingo/blob/main/plans/daily-cards-spec.md); wireframes: [`plans/daily-cards-wireframes.html`](https://github.com/nathanjohnpayne/gaycruisebingo/blob/main/plans/daily-cards-wireframes.html). Tickets are labelled `phase-1.5` and grouped Wave 0–3 (must-haves in Waves 0–2; nice-to-haves in Wave 3, shippable mid-cruise).
